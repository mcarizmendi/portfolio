Environmental Portraits

taken on: 10/20/2024


Environmental Portraiture: Mixture of portraiture and landscape where the focus is on the subject and their environment.
Saw examples from: Alec soth, Richard Renaldi, Joel Sternfeld, Steve McCurry, Philip-Lorca diCorcia, Rineke Dijkstra, Tina Barney, Larry Sultan, David LaChapelle, Gregory Crewdson, and Katy Grannan.
I decided to go into NYC, more specificaly Chelsea Flea Market and take pictures there since the surroundings environment is already so rich and different everytime.
