Abstraction

taken on: 09/22/2024


Abrstraction: super close in detail, patterns, blurs, movement, long explosure.
Saw examples from: Matthew Brandt, Man Ray, Christopher Bucklow, Hiroshi Sugimoto, Adam Fuss use cyanotype,Aaron Siskind, Bill Armstrong infinity series,Marco Breuer and Sam Falls.
I decided to go to LES Coleman Skatepark in NYC. Since the park itself is already abstract looking with all the graffiti, I decided to play with the shutter speed in order to make the skaters look blurred with movement like a stroke of paint.
